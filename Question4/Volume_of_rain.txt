***INPUT***

get inches_of_rain(float)

get lenght_of_roof(float)
get width_of_roof(float)


***PROCESS***

*convert_to_inches

width(float) = width_of_roof x 12
lenght(float) = lenght_of_roof  x 12

roof_dimension(float) = width  x  lenght

water_volume = roof_dimension X inches_of_rain
gallons = water_volume / 231

print gallons
